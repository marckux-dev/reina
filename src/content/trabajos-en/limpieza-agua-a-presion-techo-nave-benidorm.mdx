## High-Pressure Water vs Years of Grime

Damp, mould, construction residue and dirt accumulated over years. Everything that seems impossible to remove is tackled with technical pressure washing. In this job at an industrial warehouse in Benidorm, the ceiling had built up years of deposits that no conventional method would have resolved.

## Industrial Cleaning Process with Professional Kärcher Equipment

We combine pressure, the right product and, above all, patience and experience on every pass. It is not just about applying pressurised water: selecting the right flow rate, temperature and degreasing or biocidal product for the type of dirt makes the difference between a mediocre result and a completely restored surface.

The result: restored surfaces, renewed ceilings and satisfied clients.
