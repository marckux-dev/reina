## Complete Floor Restoration Process

Travertine marble has a singular beauty, but also a porosity that makes it especially vulnerable over time. In this property in Altea's old town we carried out a full restoration in several stages:

- Grinding to remove surface imperfections and level the floor
- Filling and re-grouting of holes, open joints and deteriorated areas
- Progressive polishing to recover the uniformity and natural character of the stone
- Water-repellent treatment to protect against wear and moisture

## A Finish that Respects the History of the Floor

The matte finish is not a coincidence — it is the choice that best suits the architecture of a historic property. It enhances the texture and natural veining of the travertine without adding an artificial gloss that would look out of place.

The water-repellent treatment also makes daily cleaning easier and extends the life of the floor without altering its appearance.

Every floor has its own story. We make sure to give it back all its value.
