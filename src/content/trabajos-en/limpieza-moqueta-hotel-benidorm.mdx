## The Challenge: vinyl carpet with ingrained dirt in a high-traffic area

MedPlaya Hotel Río Park commissioned us to carry out deep cleaning of its braided PVC vinyl wall-to-wall carpets, installed in corridors and high-frequency walkways. This type of material accumulates grease, ingrained dust and organic residues that conventional cleaning methods cannot eliminate. Before the intervention, the visible result was a clear loss of the original colour and a compacted texture.

Carpet cleaning in Benidorm hotels requires both professional equipment and knowledge of the material: an incorrect technique can damage the PVC fibres or leave detergent residues that attract dirt even faster.

## Process: degreasing with rotary machine and dry absorption

We applied a degreaser specific to braided PVC, allowing the necessary dwell time for it to penetrate the weave of the material. We then worked with a professional rotary machine to dislodge the dirt without damaging the fibres. The released dirt is absorbed in the same process, preventing it from being redistributed across the surface.

This industrial cleaning protocol in Benidorm allows large areas to be treated with a uniform result, without prolonged drying times that would affect hotel operations.

## Result: original colour restored, texture renewed

The difference is visible from the first treated corridor. The braided PVC recovers its natural colour and texture, with a uniform, mark-free finish. Ingrained stains disappear and the surface is left free of product residues.

- Complete elimination of ingrained dirt and stains
- PVC texture restored without damage to the fibres
- Uniform result across the entire treated surface
- No drying times that would disrupt hotel activity
