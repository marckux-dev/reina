The Flamingo Oasis Hotel in Benidorm entrusted Reina Multiservicios with the maintenance and cleaning of its large glazed surfaces, including the main façade, skylights and panoramic areas. A job that combines specialised technique at height, careful planning and the utmost care to avoid disrupting hotel operations.

## Cleaning Large Glazed Façades with an Elevated Platform

To work on surfaces of this scale we use approved elevated platforms, which allow us to access any point on the façade with precision without the need for scaffolding. This system guarantees a uniform finish on curtain walls, panoramic glazing and skylights, minimising intervention times and without interfering with the establishment's operations or its guests' experience.

## Periodic Window Maintenance for Hotels on the Costa Blanca

In prime hotel environments such as Benidorm, glass cleanliness is not merely aesthetic — it is part of the guest experience. We design periodic maintenance plans tailored to each installation, ensuring that the transparency and shine of glazed surfaces are maintained consistently throughout the year, regardless of weather conditions or occupancy levels.

## Qualified Technical Team for Work at Height

Our team consists of technicians qualified in work at height, with specific training in safety protocols and the use of professional lifting systems. The result: streak-free finishes, maximum transparency and glass preservation, even in hard-to-reach areas.

We take care of every detail so your guests see only perfection.
