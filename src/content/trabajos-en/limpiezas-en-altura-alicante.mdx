The Grand Hotel Luxor Terra Mítica entrusted Reina Multiservicios with the cleaning of windows in hard-to-reach areas of their facilities. A job that required specialised lifting machinery and a team with proven experience in high-level interventions.

## Specialised Lifting Machinery for Every Situation

To access the most complex points of this hotel we operated with our spider crane, articulated platforms and scissor lifts. This combination of equipment allows us to adapt to any spatial geometry: high ceilings, narrow corridors, glazed lobbies or façades with overhangs. No scaffolding, no temporary structures, no interruptions to the establishment.

## Work at Height with Complete Safety

Safety is non-negotiable in any high-level work. Our team operates with specific training in the use of professional lifting systems and safety protocols for hotel environments. Each intervention is planned to guarantee the result without compromising hotel operations or the guest experience.

## Impeccable Results in Demanding Environments

From the entrance hall to the most inaccessible areas, the result of our intervention at the Grand Hotel Luxor Terra Mítica speaks for itself: streak-free glass, surfaces with maximum transparency and a finish that reflects the quality of the establishment. Professionalism, safety and impeccable results on every job.
