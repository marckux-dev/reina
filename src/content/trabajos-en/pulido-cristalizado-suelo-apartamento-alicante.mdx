## Polishing, Diamond Grinding and Crystallising: Complete Restoration Process

When a marble floor has accumulated years of use, construction stains and scratches, the solution is not to replace it — it is to restore it. In this Alicante apartment we carried out a complete process that returns the floor to its original condition, with a result that goes beyond aesthetics:

- Level grinding of tiles and joints
- Removal of scratches and construction residues
- Repair of joints, breaks and holes
- High-gloss crystallising

## A Result that Improves Both Aesthetics and Durability

Crystallising is not just about shine. It creates a protective layer that hardens the surface, makes daily cleaning easier and considerably extends the life of the floor. A complete process that not only transforms the appearance of the floor but also its resistance to wear and its long-term maintenance.
