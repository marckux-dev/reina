faqs:
  - question: 'What is the difference between polishing, buffing and crystallising?'
    answer: |
      - **Polishing**: removes imperfections, scratches and unevenness to restore the original appearance of the floor, whether newly laid or worn over time.
      - **Buffing**: adds shine using high-speed rotary machines and long-lasting chemical crystallisers, either for maintenance or to renew the crystallised finish.
      - **Crystallising**: creates a protective, shiny layer on the floor surface, increasing its resistance and durability.

At Reina Multiservicios we specialise in floor polishing and crystallising in Alicante and the Costa Blanca. We work on marble, terrazzo, granite, hydraulic mosaic and natural stone, restoring the original shine of each surface through mechanical grinding, diamond polishing and crystallising processes. Our treatments are particularly in demand at homeowner communities, hotels, commercial premises, tourist apartments and high-end properties where the quality of the floor finish makes all the difference.

Marble polishing in Alicante is one of our most requested services. Through floor buffing and crystallising we apply a protective layer that not only restores the original appearance of the floor but makes it more resistant to wear and easier to maintain day to day. The process eliminates scratches, ingrained stains and the accumulated wear from foot traffic, giving the floor a like-new feel. For terrazzo or granite floors we apply equivalent techniques adapted to each material, including re-grouting where necessary.

The Alicante area has a large presence of limestone, hydraulic mosaic and other indigenous natural stones in all types of buildings. We have experience in polishing, buffing and restoring these materials, which have very different characteristics from conventional marble and require specific products and techniques. If your floor is granite, hydraulic mosaic or local stone, consult us before starting any treatment.

For hotels and communities on the Costa Blanca with large high-traffic surfaces, we also offer periodic maintenance plans for polished floors. These scheduled interventions extend the life of the crystallised finish, reduce the cost of a full restoration and ensure that common areas are always in perfect condition. We regularly work in La Nucia, Altea, Calpe, Benidorm and Alicante city, serving both private owners with luxury homes and communities and hotels with marble or terrazzo floors. We carry out no-obligation technical visits to assess the condition of the floor and prepare a quote tailored to your needs.
