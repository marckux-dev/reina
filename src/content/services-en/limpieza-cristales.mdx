At Reina Multiservicios we provide window cleaning services in Alicante, Benidorm and the entire Costa Blanca with streak- and residue-free results. We work with osmosis-treated water, which removes lime and minerals before use, preventing the whitish haze so common in the area — especially in municipalities with particularly hard water such as Benidorm, La Nucia and Altea. The result is clean glass that stays cleaner for much longer without the build-up of limescale deposits.

We have telescopic poles and approved elevated platforms for window cleaning at height, allowing us to work on façades of residential buildings, hotels, offices and commercial premises without the need for scaffolding. We serve both homeowner communities with glazed common areas and hotel sector businesses that need periodic maintenance of their glazed façades. For tall buildings with complex access, our team can also use vertical work techniques.

The hard water in the region is one of the main enemies of exterior glass on the Costa Blanca. Limescale marks build up with every rain shower or irrigation cycle, and over time can actually etch the glass if not treated correctly. Our technique using osmosis-purified water is the most effective solution to this problem: glass is left clean without scrubbing with aggressive chemical products and maintains that impeccable finish for longer.

We offer periodic maintenance contracts adapted to the frequency each client requires: weekly, monthly or quarterly. If you manage a hotel, an apartment building or a community with large glazed surfaces in Benidorm, Denia, Jávea, Calpe, Villajoyosa or any other Costa Blanca municipality, contact us for a no-obligation proposal tailored to your installation.
