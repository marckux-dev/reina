At Reina Multiservicios we apply specific surface treatments in Alicante and the Costa Blanca to protect, waterproof and improve the functionality of indoor and outdoor floors and cladding. We work with professional water-repellent, oil-repellent and anti-slip products, adapting each treatment to the type of material and conditions of use. A well-applied treatment can multiply the service life of a floor, reduce daily maintenance and prevent costly damage caused by moisture, oils or wear.

Water-repellent treatments are particularly relevant in the province of Alicante, where the combination of sun, sea salt and aggressive occasional rainfall rapidly deteriorates limestones, fired clay, terracotta, paving stone and other porous surfaces. We apply deep-penetration water repellents that repel water without altering the natural appearance of the material. These treatments are also an effective solution for terraces, swimming pools and natural stone façades that need protection against moisture and stains without losing their original texture.

For floors that present a risk of slipping — ramps, outdoor stairs, pool edges, wet walkways or industrial bathrooms — we apply approved anti-slip treatments that durably increase the surface's coefficient of friction. These treatments comply with the requirements of the Spanish Technical Building Code (CTE DB-SUA) and provide a technical and regulatory solution for property owners, building managers and businesses that need to certify the safety of their facilities. The result is invisible to the naked eye but completely effective.

We round out our offering with oil-repellent treatments for industrial kitchens and workshops, sealing of porous surfaces against stains and graffiti, and specialist conservation of outdoor fired clay and terracotta. If you are unsure what treatment your surface needs, do not worry: we carry out no-obligation technical visits in which we assess the material, its condition and the use it receives, and recommend the most appropriate and durable solution.
