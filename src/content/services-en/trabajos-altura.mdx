Reina Multiservicios carries out work at height in Alicante and the entire Costa Blanca, combining advanced technical resources with certified vertical work training. Our team operates with scissor lift platforms, articulated cranes and our spider crane — a compact, articulated unit that allows access to hard-to-reach areas such as atrium interiors, light wells or restricted-access spaces where a conventional platform cannot manoeuvre. This resource sets us apart from most companies in the area and allows us to resolve jobs that others cannot take on.

Façade cleaning at height is one of our most requested services, particularly in the hotel and residential environment of the Costa Blanca. We work on glass, composite, natural stone and mortar façades, adapting both the products and the access technique to each type of surface. All our high-level work is carried out in compliance with occupational risk prevention regulations, with current public liability insurance and certified personal protective equipment.

In addition to façade and window cleaning, we carry out other services at height that require specialised vertical access: cleaning of solar panels on roofs and flat roofs, building maintenance at height — façades, technical installations and elements in elevated areas — façade painting and repair, and installation of bird protection systems on cornices and canopies. We carry out vertical work in Benidorm, Altea and the entire coastal strip of the Costa Blanca, where the high concentration of tall buildings makes this type of service especially common. If your building or facility has a one-off or recurring maintenance need at height, describe the job to us and we will propose the most efficient solution.

We operate in Alicante, Benidorm, La Nucia, Altea, Calpe, Denia, Jávea, Torrevieja, Elche and across the province. To request a quote for work at height, contact us with a description of the access and the surface to be treated: with that information we can advise you on the most suitable equipment and the approximate cost of the intervention.
