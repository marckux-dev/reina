At Reina Multiservicios we provide comprehensive industrial cleaning services in Alicante and the entire Costa Blanca. We specialise in the cleaning of industrial warehouses in Alicante: factories, mechanical workshops, logistics centres, large surfaces and car parks, applying the right protocols and machinery for each environment. Our team is trained in occupational risk prevention and works with high-efficiency degreasing and descaling products, ensuring safe, hygienic facilities ready for operations.

Industrial cleaning covers everything from deep cleaning of warehouses and production areas to more specialised services such as post-construction cleaning, industrial machinery disinfection, osmosis cleaning of solar panels, and cleaning of cold rooms and water tanks. We also carry out technical cleaning in environments that require HACCP protocols, such as food industry facilities, where hygiene is not merely aesthetic but a regulatory requirement.

One of our most valuable offerings for businesses and industrial facilities is the periodic maintenance cleaning contract. This type of agreement allows interventions to be planned in advance, reduces long-term costs and keeps the facility in optimal condition at all times without depending on emergency cleans. We operate in Alicante, Novelda, Elche, Benidorm, La Nucia, Denia, Torrevieja and across the province, adapting the frequency and scope of each contract to each client's actual needs.

If you need a no-obligation industrial cleaning quote, contact us. We will visit your facility, assess the installation and propose the most efficient solution. We work both with businesses that fully outsource their cleaning and with other companies in the sector that require specialist support or advanced machinery for specific jobs.
