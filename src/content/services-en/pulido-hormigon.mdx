Reina Multiservicios offers concrete polishing and treatment services in Alicante and the Costa Blanca for industrial warehouses, car parks, workshops, logistics centres and community garages. Untreated concrete constantly generates dust, easily absorbs oil and grease stains and progressively deteriorates over time. A polishing and densifying process transforms that surface into a compact, dust-free floor that is easy to clean and has a significantly longer service life.

Our concrete polishing process combines mechanical phase-by-phase sanding with the application of lithium silicate hardeners, which penetrate the concrete and densify it from within. The result is a hard, dust-proof surface with the desired level of shine: from matte finish to high gloss. For industrial warehouses and sectors where hygiene is critical — such as the food or logistics industry — we also apply specific sealers that prevent liquid absorption and facilitate cleaning protocols.

One of the most common cases we handle is the re-treatment of existing concrete floors that have lost their properties over the years. Crack repair, levelling of deteriorated areas and application of a new densifying and sealing cycle allows the floor to be restored without demolition or a new screed. This option is more economical, faster and generates far less waste than a complete floor replacement.
